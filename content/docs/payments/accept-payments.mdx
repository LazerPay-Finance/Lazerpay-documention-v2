# Accept Payments

## Inline Checkout - Pop up

The Lazerpay popup provides a convenient and easy way for developers to accept payments on
the web. You can start receiving payments on your website with the checkout easily.


## 1. Add the Inline Checkout
Add the inline checkout to your website using a script tag, it is delivered through a reliable CDN.

<p className='bg-neu-50 p-2'>

    <br/>checkout@1.0.1/dist/index.min.js</code>{'</script>'}
</p>

## Important Note

<ul className='paragraph-2 space-y-5  my-6'>
<li>
    <code>1</code> The <code className='text-sec-700'>coin</code> field must either be “BUSD”, “DAI”, “USDT”, or “USDC”.
</li>
<li>
    <code>2</code>It’s ideal to generate a unique <code className='text-sec-700'>reference</code> from your system for every transaction to avoid
    duplicate attempts.
</li>
<li>
    <code>3</code> The <code className='text-sec-700'>accept_partial_payment</code>field is used to enforce payment completion by your customers.
    A scenario is when a customer is trying to pay for a service worth a fixed <code className='text-sec-700'>amount</code>of $100 USD.
    When <code className='text-sec-700'>accept_partial_payment</code> is set to true, the customer must pay the exact amount 
    before the funds will be sent to you. Read more about Partial Payments here.
</li>
<li>
    <code>4</code> The <code className='text-sec-700'>amount</code> field should be in your fiat currency. The currencies we support are USD, AED, 
     GBP, EUR and NGN. Please reach out to <i className='underline text-pri-300'>support@lazerpay.finance</i> if you would want us to
    support a currency.
</li>
<li>
    <code>5</code> The <code className='text-sec-700'>amount</code> during initialization is converted to the <code className='text-sec-700'>coin</code> equivalent of the currency
     specified.
</li>
</ul>

When a payment is successful, Lazerpay sends a <code>DEPOSIT_TRANSACTION</code> webhook event to 
webhook URL that you provide. It is highly recommended that you use <a href="#">webhooks</a> to confirm the 
payment status before delivering value to your customers.
