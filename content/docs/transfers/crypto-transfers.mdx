## Important things to Note
1. The <code>blockchain</code> field must be <code>"Binance Smart Chain"</code>.
2. The <code>coin</code> field must either be "BUSD", "DAI", "USDT", or "USDC".

<Alert status="warning">We only support the Binance smart chain for withdrawals. We will add more networks in the future.</Alert>
