### Important Note

1. The blockchain field must be "Binance Smart Chain".
2. The <code>from_coin</code> field must either be "BUSD", "DAI", "USDT", or "USDC".
3. The <code>to_coin</code> field must either be "BUSD", "DAI", "USDT", or "USDC".
4. The <code>amount</code> field should be in your fiat currency. The currencies we support are USD, AED, GBP, EUR and NGN. Please reach out to support@lazerpay.finance if you would want us to support a currency.
5. Also <code>to_coin</code> and <code>from_coin</code> cannot be the same.

<Alert status="warning">we only support the Binance smart chain for swaps. we will add more networks in the future.</Alert>
